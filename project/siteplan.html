EcoTravel Site Plan

Site Name

EcoTravel

This name was chosen because it clearly represents the purpose of the website: to promote eco-friendly and sustainable travel. The name is simple, memorable, and relevant to the target audience of eco-conscious travelers.

Site Purpose

The EcoTravel website will serve as a comprehensive guide for eco-conscious travelers. It will provide:

  • Information on eco-friendly destinations.
  • Recommendations for green accommodations.
  • Sustainable travel tips and resources.
  • A platform for users to share their own eco-travel experiences and tips.

Scenarios

These are questions that potential visitors might ask:

  • What are the best eco-friendly destinations for families?
  • How can I reduce my carbon footprint while traveling?
  • Where can I find green accommodations in Costa Rica?
  • What are some sustainable travel practices I can adopt?

Color Schema

The following colors will be used throughout the website:

  • Primary Color: #4CAF50 (Green) - Used for headers, buttons, and accents to reflect the eco-friendly theme.
  • Secondary Color: #333333 (Dark Gray) - Used for text and backgrounds to ensure readability and contrast.

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Headings: "Montserrat", sans-serif - A modern and clean font for headings.
  • Body Text: "Open Sans", sans-serif - A readable and versatile font for paragraphs and other text.
